Girlscript Durgapur has come up with its first event on "Introduction to UI/UX designing" in collaboration with Girlscript Hooghly.
User interface design (UI) is commonly defined as the process of designing the visual look of the product and the functionality of the interaction. In this event, students will be introduced to user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design, the role of designers, and the design process.
